About EcoVision

AI-powered environmental monitoring for a cleaner world

🔊

Noise Detection

Monitors decibel levels via microphone arrays placed at urban intersections. Triggers alerts above 85 dB sustained for over 10 seconds, protecting residents from noise-induced health effects.

💨

Air Quality

Tracks AQI using optical particle counters measuring PM2.5, PM10, CO₂, NO₂, and ozone concentrations. Data feeds WHO-standard thresholds with real-time alerts for industrial corridor zones.

🗑️

Dump & Waste

Ultrasonic fill-level sensors in landfill cells alert municipal teams when capacity exceeds 80%. Methane sniffers detect gas leaks early, reducing explosion risk and greenhouse emissions.

💧

Water Quality

pH probes, turbidity sensors, and dissolved oxygen meters deployed along river basins. Automated alerts when pH drifts outside the safe 6.5–8.5 range, indicating industrial runoff or acid rain.

🌍

Our Mission

EcoVision was founded on the belief that environmental data should be accessible, real-time, and actionable. By integrating IoT sensors, machine learning anomaly detection, and citizen reporting, we empower communities and governments to respond to pollution before it becomes irreversible. Every data point collected brings us one step closer to measurable, verifiable environmental justice.
